Everything it does

Features that hit the right note

All on-device. Your library, playlists and taste never leave your phone.

Local library scan

Finds every track on your device and organizes it by album and folder, with instant search and a persistent mini-player.

Background & lock-screen

Keeps playing when you switch apps or lock the phone, with full notification and lock-screen controls — next, previous, play/pause.

Android Auto

Shows up in your car and answers “play <song> from PrimeBeats” — it finds the track in your local library, or plays another if it isn't there. Backed by a native media-browser service.

P2P music sharing

Select songs and send them to another PrimeBeats user via your phone's share sheet (Nearby Share, Bluetooth…). Received tracks import straight into their library and stick around across updates.

Smart Radio (on-device ML)

An endless auto-queue that picks similar songs from the current track and your learned taste — with exploration and per-artist variety. Fully offline.

Equalizer & bass boost

A graphic EQ with device presets (Flat, Rock, Pop, Jazz…), per-band sliders in dB, and a dedicated bass-boost control. Settings persist across updates.

Gestures & progressive seek

Swipe the artwork to change track; double-tap and keep tapping a side to jump progressively further (+2, +2, +3, +4, +5s) with an on-screen flash.

Make it yours

Rename a song's title & artist, fetch or upload custom album art, drag to reorder playlists and the up-next queue, and hide recordings — all saved across updates.

↓ Download the latest APK
1. Download PrimeBeats.apk from the latest release on your Android phone.
2. Open it and allow “install unknown apps” for your browser or files app.
3. Launch PrimeBeats and grant the audio/media permission so it can scan your music.

Built with Expo · React Native · TypeScript · react-native-track-player · native Kotlin modules for the equalizer, Android Auto and P2P sharing. Updates keep your playlists, taste, custom art, EQ and received songs.

PrimeBeats is an open, non-commercial hobby project · source on GitHub · plays only the audio files you already own.
